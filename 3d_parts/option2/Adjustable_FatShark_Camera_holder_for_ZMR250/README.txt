# Summary

An adjustable angle camera holder for the FatShark TVL600 camera in a ZMR250 frame. It can be mounted with the camera behind or in front of the front pillars of the frame. 

The latch part on the back may come in conflict with the lower part of the frame at high angles. In this case you can simply cut the latch in two and just use the top part of it. It should still keep the camera securely in place.

You need 3 or 4 small screws 2x8mm. I used the type of screws that typically come with HobbyKing 9g servos
